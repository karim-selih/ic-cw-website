What is Charity Week?

Charity Week is an amazing annual campaign to raise money for orphans and needy children across the globe. In association with Islamic Relief, it is a week dedicated to bringing smiles to children across every continent.

search

Our aim

Our objective is simple: we raise as much money and awareness for orphans and needy children around the world so that they receive the shelter, education and care that they deserve, all whilst having fun!
