What is Charity Week?

Charity Week is an amazing annual campaign to raise money for orphans and needy children across the globe. In association with Islamic Relief, it is a week dedicated to bringing smiles to children across every continent.

search

Our aim

Our objective is simple: we raise as much money and awareness for orphans and needy children around the world so that they receive the shelter, education and care that they deserve, all whilst having fun!

How you can help

From donating your money, to dedicating your time as volunteers, there's an array of opportunity for you to get involved in Charity week this year! Like our Facebook page for all the updates on how you can join this worthwhile cause!

Events

Annual Dinner

Imperial College Charity Week invites you to our renowned flagship event - the Annual Charity Week Dinner! This is a must attend event for everyone!

Food Festival

Food Festival is where you can find a range of mouth-watering cuisines, fun activities and buzzing vibes, all located in the heart of London! 

Collects

Collects is the heart of Charity Week! Put on your CW t-shirt, grab a bucket and raise as much money as possible at street collects, tube collects and brand new this year: mosque collects!

Stalls

From chicken wings to cakes to smoothies - all available in one place, CW Stalls! Come along, experience this wonderful mix of food and have a chat and learn what CW is all about!

Big Brunch

An afternoon of food and fun as we aim to spread the message of Charity Week through mouth-watering cuisine whilst we delve into the meaning of charity with our guest performances.

Speaker Event

An evening full of eye-opening talks detailing some of the many disasters scattered across our world and what we can do to help. You do not want to miss this!
