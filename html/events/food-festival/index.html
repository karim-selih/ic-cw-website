Food Festival


Imperial College Charity Week is proud to present Food Festival 2019! With halal, vegetarian and gluten free food on offer, there's no excuse to miss this unforgettable event. This year we've lined up some great activities on Queen's lawn for all ages to revel in. Located within a short walk from hyde park and the science museum, it's the perfect family day out! Bring your friends and family down to immerse yourselves in the buzzing atmosphere and satisfy your taste buds!
