Speaker Event


The much-anticipated CW speaker event returns for 2019, with heart-warming and inspirational speakers, it is not one to miss! Join us as we strive to remember the true reasons behind why we get involved with charity and understand the daily struggles of men, women and children who are just trying to make it through each day. As we sit in our cushioned lives, help us to reflect on what Charity Week has done for these people and how we can be the next changemakers; putting a smile on these faces and many more to come.
