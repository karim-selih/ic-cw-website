What is charity week without YOU the volunteers giving up your time to raise money. That's what collects is all about, whether you're at a tube station from 7am dressed as Nemo collecting money or only have a few minutes between lectures to run out to exhibition road to start collecting, we need all of you guys to give up your time to help us raise as much money as possible. As every year, we have a day for tube collects and a day for street collects. Brand new this year we will also be organising collects at local mosques. So make sure you keep your eyes peeled on how to get involved on what is always a highlight of CW!
