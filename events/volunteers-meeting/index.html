Volunteers' Summit


Kickstarting the Charity Week Campaign is the Volunteers' Summit, where you can meet the heads, learn about the events and find out how YOU can join us on the CW journey!

Every campaign has a team of hard-working, devoted volunteers that play an indispensable role in driving it to success. Without you, Charity Week would not be able to grow into the incredible campaign it is today.

We therefore invite you all to our annual Volunteers' Summit, where all the CW secrets will be revealed, and a chance for YOU to drive this year’s success insha’Allah.
